Mehbub is an indie hacker and full stack engineer from India. He works across the full stack — **React**, **Next.js**, **TypeScript**, and modern web UI on the front; **Node.js**, **Python**, and **Flask** on the back; **PostgreSQL**, **Redis**, and **Docker** for data, jobs, and deployment.

As a Google Summer of Code 2026 contributor with UC OSPO, he is working on the [Environmental NeTworked Sensor (ENTS)](/project/osre26/ucsc/ents) / **DirtViz** project under the mentorship of {{% mention "cjosephson" %}}, [John Madden](mailto:jtmadden@ucsc.edu), and [Alec Levy](mailto:alevy1@ucsc.edu). His work focuses on a dynamic sensor dashboard: drag-and-drop layouts, URL-shareable views, parallel data loading, derived metric equations, and CSV export on the existing Flask + React stack.
